Make change happen in a simpler way: Define. Evaluate. Deliver.

Portfolio definition

See the important things

STRATEGY ALIGNMENT

Decompose your strategy in a hierarchical process and identify the initiatives that directly support your strategic objectives avoiding investments in non-value activities

PORTFOLIO HIERARCHY

Create a portfolio structure that matches the value creation accountability in your company and assign projects to the correct portfolio owner

CATEGORIZATION AND PRIORITIZATION

Categorise and prioritize your projects from multiple viewpoints and make rational portfolio balancing decisions

Portfolio evaluation

Do the right things

VALUE ANALYSIS

Select projects that will provide the best value. Evaluate your portfolio from all perspectives: financial, qualitative, risk, and interdependency

STAKEHOLDER ANALYSIS

Ensure that all the people involved are mobilized for success. Manage stakeholder expectations and their engagement on the change effort

CONTINUOUS IMPROVEMENT

Actively manage reviews and maturity improvements to continuously optimize your portfolio management process

Portfolio delivery

Do things right

GATE PERFORMANCE MANAGEMENT

Control portfolio performances and stay on track through quantitative control of baselines, estimates and actual data. Apply a flexible outcome-based gate process to project delivery while maintaining Agility.

DELIVERY MONITORING

Keep an eye on escalations and rein over fire-fighting activity to promptly remove roadblocks to the delivery of your portfolio

TEAMWORK AND ACCOUNTABILITY

Engage your team and stakeholders in the portfolio management process. Default roles and rights restrictions for PMO members, project managers, portfolio managers and executives
